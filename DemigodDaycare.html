Demigod Daycare

Mobile Autobattler

Welcome to the first place the children of the gods learn to be legendary heroes... daycare! Face other teams and prove your daycare to be the mightiest in all of Olympus.

Platform: Mobile (Apple + Android)

Role: Producer and 2D Artist

Team Size: 6

Publisher: MassDigi

Released: August 2022

Art Contributions

  • Produced background, character and user interface artwork along with one other artist.
  • Established a strong art style taking inspiration from ancient Greece and modern animations.
  • Created and managed the art pipeline.
  • Took art from concept artist and rendered it to create polished pieces.
  • Made small animations to add liveliness to gameplay.

Production Contributions

  • Directed production of mobile game over 12 weeks.
  • Used modified sprints and a kanban board to organize progress.
  • Managed the art pipeline and creation of art assets.
  • Hosted daily scrums and organized the backlog to support fluctuating scope.
  • Onboarded new workers to a pre-existing project after release.
  • Created a layered scope plan to allow room for scope adjustments.
Agile Scrum

We used two-week-long sprints and daily standups to organize production, taking inspiration from Agile Scrum.

Miro

Miro was used during online development to visualize ideas, facilitate communication between engineers and artists, and to organize tasks when the in-person kanban board was inaccessible.

Plastic SCM

The team used Plastic SCM as our source control.
