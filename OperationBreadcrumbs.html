2D Management and Narrative Game Demo

Bed and BEAKfast is a management, cooking, and narrative game. Play as Robin and return to your hometown to regrow the bed and breakfast left to you by your late grandfather. Cook meals, connect with the community, and forge lasting relationships!

The demo is free on Steam and on Itch.io!

Platform: PC (Mac + Windows)

Role: Producer and Lead Artist

Team Size: 6

Development Time: 10 months

Production Contributions

  • Organized and facilitated Scrum meetings including sprint planning, reviews, retrospectives, and stand-ups.
  • Adjusted processes to better fit the team's needs.
  • Assisted communication across disciplines within team.
  • Evaluated scope at each checkpoint, and adjusted backlog to fit long-term goals.
  • Worked with designers and engineers to prioritize features according to the project's needs.
  • Created a layered scope plan based on prioritization to iterate upon our core loop and to keep each subteam working towards the same reachable goals.
  • Evaluated existing tools for tracking tasks, adjusting them or creating new tools and methods when needed.
Agile Scrum

We employed Agile Scrum to organize development. The team found that we functioned best with two-week-long sprints.

Jira

We used Atlassian's Jira to create, organize, and point our backlog, plan for each sprint, and to track our velocity.

Trello

Trello was used to make a more detailed kanban board for the artists to better fit their pipeline.

Art Contributions

  • Lead the creation of an art style taking inspiration from influential American artists.
  • Created and managed the art pipeline.
  • Produced 2D character sprites to be rigged and animated by the team's animators.
  • Oversaw quality control of all artwork.
  • Assigned and prioritized Art tasks based on the needs and abilities of the team.
