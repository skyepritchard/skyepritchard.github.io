Hi! I'm Skye, an artist and game producer with a passion for telling stories and sharing experiences through collaboration and communication. In my time working at MassDigi, on personal projects, and in my education, I have fallen in love with the art of facilitating team processes and uplifting teammates so that they can create amazing things.

I spend my free time making art of my own, exploring every medium I can get my hands on: from writing stories and designing characters to sculpting clay figures, needle felting, and sewing.
