Game Jam Stat Raiser

Platform: PC

Role: Developer

Team Size: 2

Development Time: 9 Days

Released: July 2024

Winner of the July 2024 Godot Wild Jam, themed "unintended consequences!"

The King has given you a quest: to slay the evil dragon at the edge of the world! You are beyond honored.

However, when you get there, you realize that the so-called 'evil' dragon is only a babe! Moved with compassion for the creature, you decide to raise it instead.

Train the Dragon over the course of a year, raising its stats to surpass a variety of day-to-day challenges. Surmount unexpected consequences when you raise the Dragon incorrectly, and guide it towards ways it can assist others to help increase its moral caliber.

May you raise a dragon worthy of the King!
