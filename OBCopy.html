Operation Breadcrumbs

In-Progress 2D Management Game

Platform: PC (Mac + Windows)

Role: Producer and Lead Artist

Team Size: 6

Release: Demo coming Spring 2024

Play as Robin, a bird returning to their charming childhood town to take over their grandfather's BnB. Make breakfasts, meet migrating birds passing through, and get to know the community you left behind.
