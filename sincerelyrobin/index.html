2D Management and Narrative Game Demo

Bed and BEAKfast is a management, cooking, and narrative game. Play as Robin and return to your hometown to regrow the bed and breakfast left to you by your late grandfather. Cook meals, connect with the community, and forge lasting relationships!

An early demo titled "Bed and BEAKfast" is free on Steam and on Itch.io!

Platform: PC (Mac + Windows)

Role: Producer and Art Director

Team Size: 8

Development Time: 2 years, part-time

Agile

Due to the changing nature of the team's time commitments, we employed Agile Scrum to organize development, ensuring flexibility in both our product and our processes. The team used Scrum at first, finding that two-week sprints worked best to organize development. However, in the second year of development, the team switched to Kanban, favoring the focus on consistent workflows to be more beneficial.

Jira

We used Atlassian's Jira to create, organize, and point our backlog, plan for each sprint, and to track our velocity.

Miro

Miro's whiteboard workspace was used to visualize tasks and workflows as the developers' time commitments shifted.
