Gotta Go-Bot!

3D Platformer

Platform: PC

Role: Producer and Lead Artist

Team Size: 7

Development Time: 7 weeks

A small robot journeys to the heart of a giant humanoid contraption, seeking answers after waking up in the wilderness with no memories. Use your jump, dash, and grappling hook to get through the levels and avoid the creatures that have taken up residence.

Contributions

Art Contributions

  • Tracked art progress using a Gantt Chart.
  • Facilitated communication with Tech team using standups and meetings.
  • Lead and contributed to the creation of concept art to establish game style and mood.
  • Designed, modeled, UV Mapped, and textured game assets alongside two artists, based on concept art and level design.
  • Made small animations to add liveliness to gameplay.
