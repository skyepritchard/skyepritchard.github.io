(Game Jam) Management Game

Platform: PC

Role: Developer

Team Size: 2

Development Time: 9 Days

Released: October 2025

2nd place in the October 20254 Godot Wild Jam, themed "Masks."

All you want to do is do your job of making illegal money illegally. Your coffee shop is just a front, a MASK, it's not supposed to actually get customers!

Toe the line in this management game by forging money and serving customers at the same time! Work hard enough to get good ratings and avoid suspicion, but don't forget to actually make your illegal money... lest you get fired!
